MOST POPULAR BOOKS OF ALL TIME

1.

Where the Crawdads Sing

by Delia Owens

Summary: A #1 New York Times Best Seller and Reese Witherspoon Book Club pick,
Where the Crawdads Sing is at once an exquisite ode to the natural world,
a heartbreaking coming-of-age story, and a surprising tale of possible murder.
Owens reminds us that we are forever shaped by the children we once were,
and that we are all subject to the beautiful and violent secrets that nature keeps.
